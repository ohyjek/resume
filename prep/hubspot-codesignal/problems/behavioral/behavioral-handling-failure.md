## Prompt (normalized)

Tell me about a time you failed and what you did afterward.

## What it is really asking

- Self-awareness.
- Learning velocity.
- Accountability.

## Input/Output expectations

- Input: STAR story with clear failure and recovery.
- Output: concrete lessons and changed behavior.

## Constraints and edge cases

- Avoid stories with no real stake.
- Avoid blaming others.

## Solution paths

1. STAR with explicit reflection segment.

## Recommended baseline approach

Choose a meaningful failure, own the mistake, show corrected process.

## Complexity

- N/A (behavioral)

## Common pitfalls

- Disguised success story.
- Weak or generic lessons.

## Follow-up variants

- Failure with team communication breakdown.

## Practice checklist

- [ ] Clear "what I would do differently" statement.
- [ ] Measurable change after failure.
